# Acme SaaS — SLA & Insurance

## Service Level Agreement (SLA)

### Uptime Commitments
- **Business Plan**: 99.9% monthly uptime (approx. 43 minutes downtime/month).
- **Enterprise Plan**: 99.95% monthly uptime (approx. 22 minutes downtime/month).

### SLA Credits
If Acme SaaS fails to meet the uptime commitment, customers are eligible for service credits:
- 99.0% – 99.9% uptime: 10% credit on next invoice.
- 95.0% – 99.0% uptime: 25% credit on next invoice.
- Below 95.0% uptime: 50% credit on next invoice.

Credits must be requested within 30 days of the incident and do not exceed 50% of the
monthly subscription fee.

### Exclusions
SLA credits do not apply to downtime caused by:
- Customer actions or misconfigurations.
- Force majeure events.
- Scheduled maintenance (communicated 72 hours in advance).
- Third-party service outages (e.g., AWS, identity providers).

## Cyber Liability Insurance

### Coverage
Acme SaaS maintains the following insurance policies:
- **Cyber Liability Insurance**: $5M per occurrence / $10M aggregate.
  Covers data breach response costs, regulatory fines, and third-party claims.
- **Technology Errors & Omissions (E&O)**: $5M per claim.
  Covers professional liability arising from service failures.
- **General Commercial Liability**: $2M per occurrence.

### Certificates
Certificates of Insurance (COI) are available upon request from legal@acmesaas.com.
Additional insured endorsements can be provided for Enterprise customers under contract.
