# Acme SaaS — Compliance & Certifications

## Active Certifications
Acme SaaS currently maintains the following certifications and attestations:

- **SOC 2 Type II**: Annual audit covering Security and Availability trust principles.
  Most recent report dated 2025-09. Available under NDA from trust@acmesaas.com.
- **ISO/IEC 27001:2022**: Certified since 2024. Certificate available on request.
- **GDPR**: Acme SaaS acts as a Data Processor under GDPR Article 28. A signed DPA
  with Standard Contractual Clauses is available.
- **CCPA**: Acme SaaS complies with the California Consumer Privacy Act for applicable
  customer data.

## Certifications NOT Held
The following frameworks are commonly asked about but Acme SaaS does NOT currently hold:

- **HIPAA**: Acme SaaS is NOT HIPAA certified and does NOT sign Business Associate
  Agreements (BAAs). Customers must not store Protected Health Information (PHI) on
  the platform.
- **PCI DSS**: Acme SaaS is NOT a PCI-DSS certified processor. Payment data is handled
  via Stripe; raw cardholder data never touches Acme SaaS systems.
- **FedRAMP**: Acme SaaS is NOT FedRAMP authorized.
- **IRAP / Australian Government**: Not certified.

## Penetration Testing
Acme SaaS engages an independent third party for annual penetration testing. Executive
summary reports are available under NDA. Bug bounty program is run via HackerOne.
